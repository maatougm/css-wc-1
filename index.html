About

me Vincent, the fiery Dutchman with a paintbrush for a tongue. I toiled in obscurity, painting peasants and cafes, but the sun-drenched magic of Arles unlocked my true voice. Sunflowers bathed in gold, starry nights whispered secrets, each stroke a burst of my soul. Demons danced, the path was lonely, but beauty bloomed in faces and fields. Each brushstroke sang of joy, sorrow, and life's raw truth. Recognition was a fickle friend, but my legacy? Now it roars! Come, step into my vibrant world, where colors dance and emotions sing. This is Vincent van Gogh, forever yours.
